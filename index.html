    About
    Who We Are

    VOID is a Berlin-based design studio working at the intersection of brand identity, digital design, and editorial.

    We believe in restraint as a strategy. Every element must justify its existence. We remove what doesn't serve the message, keeping only what communicates with clarity.

    Our work spans visual identity systems, digital experiences, and exhibitions — always guided by the same principle: subtract until only truth remains.
